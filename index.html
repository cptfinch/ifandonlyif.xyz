How it works

1

Read the mathematics

Each exercise explains the concept — what the theorem means, why it matters, and the intuition behind it.

2

Step through the proof

Watch the proof state transform at each tactic. See hypotheses appear, goals narrow, subgoals split. Understand why each step works.

3

Prove it yourself

Fill in the proof skeleton. Paste into Lean 4 Web or your local VS Code. If Lean accepts it, you've proved it.
